Tuition

Tuition is calculated per session, at $15 for each one-hour class. Sessions may be of various lengths, from 8 to 10 classes. So your tuition will be determined when you sign up for a session, and is due in full at that time.

Payment FAQs

Can I pay in installments?

No. Tuition must be paid in full at the start of a session.

Do I get a refund for missing a class?

No. Because the tuition is for the entire session, not for individual classes, no refunds or credits are offered if a class is missed.
